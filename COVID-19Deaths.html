Where new deaths are increasing

Tooltips and filters are available. Use your mouse to hover over any bars on the bar chart to see its tooltip, and drill-down combobox to filter the information by state.

COVID-19 has killed tens of thousands of Americans from Guam to the continental United States to the U.S. Virgin Islands. U.S. states and territories on July 22nd reported more than 1,100 new deaths attributed to the novel coronavirus, marking the first time since May 29 that the country exceeded that number. The tally was led by Texas, which reported a record 197 new deaths Wednesday. Deaths from the virus have continued to climb upward since June, a troubling trend that gives credence to experts who expected an increase in fatalities following the recent surge in infections.
